🧳 Packing list

Mid-October — cool, layered, a little rain

  • Comfortable walking shoes (lots of cobblestones)
  • Warm layers: sweater + light coat or jacket
  • Scarf and a compact umbrella / rain layer
  • Swimwear + flip-flops (the thermal baths!)
  • Sunglasses for crisp autumn days
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Personal medication + copy of prescriptions
  • Power bank and cables
  • EU power adapter (type C/F)
  • Some cash in Forint for small cafés & markets

📄 Documents

Check a week before the flight

  • Passports valid 6+ months past return date
  • Travel & health insurance (policy number saved)
  • Flight confirmation — code YIZU8L
  • Apartment address: Síp utca 16–18, District VII
  • Restaurant reservations (Carmel, etc.)
  • Digital copies of all documents on your phone
  • Emergency & insurance contact numbers

💶 Money & tipping

Currency: Hungarian Forint (HUF)

  • Currency Forint (Ft / HUF)
  • Cards Accepted almost everywhere
  • Cash Handy for markets & small cafés
  • Restaurant tip ~10–12%
  • Check the bill Service sometimes already added
  • ATMs Use bank ATMs; decline "conversion"

🌤️ Weather

Mid-October in Budapest

  • Daytime highs 15–17°C
  • Evenings / nights 7–9°C, chilly
  • Rain A few showers likely
  • Daylight Sunset ~17:45

So: layers for the day, a warm jacket for the evening Danube cruise.
